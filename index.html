About Me

★

Hello, I'm Samantha!

I'm a Frontend Software Developer who's passionate about design, coding, and helping people.

Before making the switch into web development, I spent 7 years in different industries, sharpening my marketing and communication skills. I've had the privilege of working for the City of Toronto where I collaborated with public officials to create innovative solutions that benefit the community. I've also contributed my skills to a dynamic startup where I played a crucial role in their marketing efforts as well as customer success management. Additionally, I dedicated my time and expertise to a non-profit organization, where I focused on design and community empowerment.

By integrating my design, customer success, and people skills into the tech industry and web development, I bring a user-centered touch to my work. This unique blend of skills plays a crucial role in the creation of solutions that not only meets technical specs but also surpass the expectations of our end-users. This creates a more user-friendly and enjoyable digital experience that's built to last.

I'm excited to keep exploring new horizons in the tech world and continuing to learn from every challenge that comes my way.

Thanks for stopping by, and feel free to reach out!
